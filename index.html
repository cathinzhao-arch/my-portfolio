Catherine Zhao

A Filmmaker Bridging Worlds

"My passion for cinema lies in its power to translate complex human emotions into universal images. Whether I am behind the camera or analyzing a script, I am dedicated to exploring stories that connect different cultures and perspectives."

Visual Montages (照相蒙太奇)

A collection of narrative photography where still images are sequenced to tell a complete cinematic story.

My Observer (我的观察者)

A man, desperate to find his missing girlfriend, begins stalking her best friend in the iconic red dress. His obsession leads him down a path toward a terrifying truth he never expected.

A Misunderstanding of the Wind (一场风的误会)

When the wind steals a girl’s bookmark, a kind stranger retrieves it, sparking an instant, one-sided love. However, her romantic daydreams are quickly shattered by a heartbreaking reality.

Performance Highlights

Crazy Rich Asians (Adapted Scene)

Role: Polyn (The Housekeeper)

Inspired by the original film, we reimagined this story through the mother's perspective. I portrayed Polyn, the kind-hearted and energetic maid of the Yang family, bringing warmth and life to the household dynamics.

The Holy Spirits

Role: Kate

A comedy short following the bond between best friends Grace and Kate. I played Kate—the quintessential party girl and loyal friend who balances the duo with her vibrant energy.

Sober (清醒梦)

A poignant short film exploring the journey of moving on. Through a "lucid dream" lens, it captures the beautiful highs of a romantic love story and the devastating reality of a breakup.

Solitude (孤独)

A sad short film focused on the often-ignored type of loneliness—the kind felt even when surrounded by the wrong group of friends. It explores the isolation of being misunderstood by those closest to you.

In Development

Le ciel te regarde (The Sky is Watching You)

I am honored to be part of this new project. It is a mystical narrative about a grieving man who finds a spiritual connection to his late wife when he is reincarnated as an eagle.
