A Filmmaker Bridging Worlds
"My passion for cinema lies in its power to translate complex human emotions into universal images. Whether I am behind the camera or analyzing a script, I am dedicated to exploring stories that connect different cultures and perspectives."

Visual Montages (照相蒙太奇)

A narrative technique where curated still images are sequenced to create a cinematic flow. By focusing on composition and the silent space between frames, I explore how visual rhythm can tell a complete story without the need for dialogue.

Performance Highlights

Crazy Rich Asians: A Reimagined Adaptation

Role: Polyn (Housekeeper)

"This performance piece is a creative reimagining of the global hit, shifted to explore the narrative through the mother’s point of view. I portrayed Polyn, the Yang family’s dedicated housekeeper. In this role, I focused on bringing a vibrant, kind-hearted, and energetic spirit to the screen, serving as a vital emotional pulse within the complex world of the Yang household."
